Elizabeth's Handbags

Elizabeth's Handbags is a 3rd party, "in-between" E-commerce site. All of our bags are made with authentic leather from the most respected retailers. Each bag comes without a designer label but restains the famous "look" of the purse it is inspired by.

About Us

In addition to providing luxury handbags at a generous discounted price, we at Elizabeth's Handbags double as an accessory look-book blog account. In accompany of each of our bags, we will provide fashion tips and ideas for how to accessorize your purse with everyday fashion sense. We believe a "completed" outfit has the power to empower those who share our love for authentic leather bags. Pictured is a crocodile embossed "So Kelly" inspired back. As our standard, it is genuine leather and broken in, ensuring only the most natural experience for our customers (Non-broken in or stiff bags are for the birds!). For those who like a pop of color to their wardobe can add an interchangable strap. Pictured is an African print themed strap.

In addition to providing non-name brand purses, (but still high-quality!) are gently used purses. As pictured above, this as an authentic LV backpack. Leather is broken in with without any wears are tears. That being said, an upcoming project we have are the sell of non-name brand (don't worry, still high-quality!) wallets. As stated above, there would be an option of stylized wallets being apart of look-books. It's important to stay in style even when you're pulling out your checkbook! Pictured is a hot-pink wallet with gold hardware.
